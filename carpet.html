FLOOR MAT

Selector Guide

  • Vacuum daily. Periodically deep clean with carpet extractor using water based detergent or shampoo., & deck brush.
  • Do not use solvent based cleaners
  • Allow mats to dry completely before returning to service.
  • Roll mat with pile facing outward to store and never stand rolled mats on end.
